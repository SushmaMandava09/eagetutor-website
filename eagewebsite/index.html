About Us

Welcome to eAgetutor, a digital English and skill development training company dedicated to helping aspiring English speakers achieve their goals. With a strong emphasis on quality education, we have trained over 250,000 students with the assistance of a panel of 5000 teachers. Our platform boasts more than 1 million registered users and has conducted 2.5 million sessions on our Learning Management System.

Our vision is to be the best-in-class digital English and skill development portal, improving the lives of millions of deserving customers. We offer a range of products in communication skills and skill development training, catering to individual students as well as corporate clients. With our customized learning solutions, we help users gain confidence and prosper in their careers.

At eAgetutor, we take pride in our understanding of ground realities, relentless focus on customer satisfaction, and ability to conduct large-scale, multiple-location training services with flawless execution. Our management team, led by CEO and Founder Chander K. Madan, brings decades of experience and expertise to drive our mission forward.
